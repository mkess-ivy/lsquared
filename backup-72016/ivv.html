Independent Verification & Validation

The old adage of “trust but verify” is the foundation on which our IV&V service is based for validating a system’s capabilities and performance.Our Test Engineers have over 10 years of experience conducting IV&V activities from simple applications to complex system such as satellite control systems. Working in tandem with system engineers and architects, we gain in-depth knowledge of the system performance and its expected behaviors. The methods employed by our Test Engineers ensure a high degree of integrity, repeatability, and accountability.

Independent Verification & Validation consists of the following:
  • Test Plan Development
  • Test Procedure Development & Execution for the following types of tests:
    • Day in the Life Testing (DITL) or functional
    • Acceptance
  • Verification Trace Matrix Development
  • Report Generation
  • Planning and Scheduling
  • Tool Development
  • Test Data Development
