Life at L Squared

Why Work at LSquared?

L Squared is professional services company focused on provided IT Security Consulting to Federal Government and Commercial Organizations. L Squared offers competitive wages and benefits to our employees. L Squared is continuously focused on attracting and retaining exceptional individuals who are looking to work at a different kind of company: people who share our values, who thrive in a team environment, and who recognize the importance of accountability.

L Squared believes that retention of our employees is critical to our success as well as the success of our clients. Our benefits are designed to be flexible; because we know each one of our employees have unique needs. We offer a range of benefit packages to ensure that everyone - have options in health, savings and other important areas that support a work & life balance. We are always looking into ways to improve our benefits package for our employees.

LSquared offers the following benefits:

  • Competitive Pay
  • Medical and Dental Coverage
  • 401(k) and Profit Sharing
  • Personal Time Off
  • Flexible Work Schedule
  • Telecommuting

Why Choose a Career with LSquared?

L Squared is always looking for energetic, self-starting, technical professionals who want to work in a small but growing business. We need employees who are looking for a long term commitment to an organization that values them as individuals and their growth as professionals. If you are looking for challenging work, professional growth, and an opportunity to work in a challenging and dynamic work environment please review our current job opportunities and consider submitting your resume for a career at L Squared, LLC.
